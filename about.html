About Us

Founded in 2010, Tefanie's Bakery has become a local favorite for fresh, handcrafted pastries. What started as a passion for baking in a small family kitchen has grown into a full-service bakery known for its warm ambiance, friendly staff, and mouth-watering treats.

Our mission is to bring a little bit of happiness to everyone who walks through our door. Whether you're here for your daily bread, a weekend treat, or a special occasion cake, our bakers are committed to quality and joy in every bite.
